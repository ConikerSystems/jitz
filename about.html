What Jitz Does

Jitz is a personal study tool for the foundational Gracie Combatives curriculum — the essential self-defense techniques of Royce Gracie Jiu-Jitsu. Each technique lists its position and category, and the curated lessons link to a verified instructional video you can watch right inside the app.

Mark each move as Not started, Started, or Completed as you work through it, and rate the videos you watch (1–5 stars) to build a list of the instructors you learn best from.

Private & Offline

Jitz installs to your home screen and the interface works offline (the videos themselves still need an internet connection). Your progress — Not started, Started, or Completed — is saved on this device only; there is no account and no server.

And because it's privately developed by Coniker Systems™, there are no ads — ever.

Get Jitz on Your iPad

It's a free web app — nothing to buy, no App Store needed. To keep it on your iPad or iPhone so it opens like a normal app and works offline:

  1. In Safari (you're likely here already), tap the Share button — the square with an arrow.
  2. Choose “Add to Home Screen.”
  3. Tap Add — a Jitz icon appears on your home screen.
  4. Open it from the home screen. It runs full-screen and works offline.

Getting New Features

New moves and features are added regularly. To get the latest version, just tap 🔄 Update on the home screen while connected to Wi-Fi — it refreshes itself, and your progress is kept. You can check the version number at the bottom of any screen.

Have an idea or found a problem? Tap 💬 Feedback on the home screen to send it straight to us — your feedback shapes what gets built next.
