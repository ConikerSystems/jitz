Jitz

Study Royce Gracie Jiu-Jitsu — browse the Gracie Combatives curriculum and watch each move.

Jitz is a personal study tool for the foundational Gracie Combatives curriculum — the 36 essential self-defense techniques of Royce Gracie Jiu-Jitsu. Each technique lists its position and category, and the curated lessons link to a verified instructional video you can watch right in the app.

Jitz installs to your home screen and the interface works offline (the videos themselves still need an internet connection). Your progress — Not started, Started, or Completed — is saved on this device only; there is no account and no server.
